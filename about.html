§01.1 — Position

One job, done well.

The practice ends at the construction certificate. No tender. No construction administration. Drafting with one job — and we do it well.

That focus is deliberate. NSW approval pathways have their own logic: the documentation a council expects for a DA is not the same documentation a private certifier wants for a CC. Working at the seam between them — daily — produces faster turnaround and fewer RFIs.

Most of our work is for commercial fitout proponents — café and retail tenancies in the 50–250 m² range, primarily on Sydney's Northern Beaches and surrounding LGAs. Clients are typically tenants, designers, or builders who need approval-grade documentation without the overhead of a full architecture engagement.

We work in Revit 2024 against NCC 2022, with a custom family library tuned for hospitality and retail — accessible amenities, food premises against AS 4674, signage, mezzanines.

§01.2 — Principal

Run by Michel Toubia.

Michel is the principal drafter and sole point of contact at DPM. Every drawing, report, and lodgement leaves the studio under his name.

Trained in architectural drafting and operating as a sole practitioner under MT Management Pty Ltd, Michel works across the full approval pathway — concept to lodgement to CC. The practice was built around a deliberate scope: small commercial fitouts, NSW only, drafted for approval and nothing else.

Outside DPM, Michel runs short-term rental properties on the side and develops StayOps, a property management platform he originally built for his own use.
