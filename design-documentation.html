01
Design & Documentation

We design and document residential, commercial and industrial projects for approval — whether that's a Development Application (DA) with council, a Complying Development Certificate (CDC) through a certifier, or Construction Certificate (CC) documentation to get construction started. Come to us with a sketch on a napkin, a set of plans from another consultant, or an existing DA approval — we handle the drawings, the pathway, the consultant coordination and the submission.

What we design

By sector
Residential

Residential

Houses, duplexes, granny flats and other residential accommodations. We take your vision and turn it into a documented, approval-ready design that works within council controls.

Commercial

Commercial

Cafés, restaurants, medical centres, service stations, retail fitouts and change of use projects. Designed to meet BCA, accessibility, food premises and other statutory requirements from the start.

Industrial

Industrial

Warehouses, factories and industrial facilities. Documentation prepared for the relevant approval pathway with fire, egress and structural provisions considered at design stage.

How we get it approved

By pathway
DA

Development Application

We design within the LEP and DCP controls that apply to your site and prepare the full documentation set for council assessment. The goal is a clean submission that moves through assessment without unnecessary delays or RFIs.

CDC

Complying Development Certificate

Not every project needs a DA. We check your planning certificate and liaise with certifiers to confirm whether your project qualifies for the faster CDC pathway before any documentation work begins — so you're not paying for the wrong process.

CC

Construction Certificate

Already have a DA approval? We prepare the CC documentation so construction can commence — whether we did the original DA or another consultant did. We review the conditions of consent, update the drawings and coordinate with the certifier.

What else is included

Beyond drawings

Consultant coordination

We source quotes from engineers, surveyors and other specialists, review their reports, and implement their requirements into the drawing set — so you're not chasing five different consultants yourself.

Submission

We lodge the application with the assessing authority (council or certifier), including all required documentation, declarations and fees.

Reference
Approval pathways

Common approval pathways.

A short reference to the pathways most often referenced in our work. The right route depends on the project, site and council — we recommend the most suitable option once the brief is reviewed.

DA

Development Application

Formal planning approval from council — used for change of use, new works, alterations and signage.

CDC

Complying Development Certificate

Faster, certifier-issued approval available when a project meets the conditions of the relevant SEPP.

CC

Construction Certificate

Issued after DA approval — confirms documentation complies with the BCA and conditions of consent. Authorises construction to commence.

OC

Occupation Certificate

Issued at the end of construction — confirms the building is suitable for occupation.
