§01 — Practice

A drafting room tuned for the approval pathway.

We work on the documentation that gets a project approved. Floor plans drawn for council. Sections that resolve compliance. Schedules that pre-empt the RFI.

Drafting through to lodgement, on every approval pathway NSW offers — DA, CDC, and the Construction Certificate that follows.

DPM Design and Project Management is run from Sydney's north-west by Michel Toubia. The practice was set up to serve a specific gap: small commercial fitout proponents who need approval-grade documentation without the overhead of a full architecture engagement.

Most projects are café and retail tenancies on the Northern Beaches, working alongside designers, builders, and certifiers from brief through to portal lodgement. Documentation is produced in Revit 2024 against NCC 2022.
