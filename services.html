§ 02 — Services
Six deliverables · NSW only

Six deliverables, end to end.

From drafting through to Construction Certificate, every output your project needs to clear an NSW approval pathway. Engaged à la carte or as a complete package — most projects use the full set.

← Practice · Projects →
§ 02.1

Architectural drafting

Revit 2024 documentation for commercial fitouts. Floor plans, sections, elevations, RCPs, joinery, schedules. Drawn to a documentation standard that pre-empts the certifier's RFI before it gets sent.

Drafting can be engaged on its own — for as-built records, internal coordination drawings, or marketing — or as the foundation for the approval pathway services that follow.

  • Tenancy survey and base build verification
  • Floor plans, RCPs, sections, elevations
  • Joinery elevations and details
  • Finishes, fixtures, and equipment schedules
  • Custom Revit family library tuned for hospitality and retail
Deliverable: Coordinated Revit model, PDF drawing set, schedules
§ 02.2

Development applications

Full DA documentation for fitouts where merit assessment is required — change of use, Class 6 conversions, signage outside exempt thresholds, and works that can't be picked up under CDC.

The submission is built around the Statement of Environmental Effects. Drawings are coordinated to the SEE, the SEE is coordinated to the LEP, and the package is coordinated to the council's specific lodgement checklist.

  • Site analysis and existing tenancy plans
  • Proposed plans, sections, elevations
  • Statement of Environmental Effects (SEE)
  • Plan of Management where required
  • Signage schedule and street elevations
  • Coordination with consultants (acoustic, traffic, hydraulic)
Deliverable: DA-ready package · Lodged via NSW Planning Portal
§ 02.3

Complying development

CDC-pathway documentation for fitouts that comply with the Commercial & Industrial Code. Faster than DA — but only if the assessment is right and every checklist line is pre-empted.

We carry out the compliance assessment up front so the pathway choice is made on evidence, not optimism. If the project doesn't comply, we say so — and pivot to DA before fees are wasted on the wrong submission.

  • Code compliance assessment against the relevant SEPP
  • Floor plans, sections, elevations to CDC standard
  • Compliance statements clause-by-clause
  • Coordination with the appointed certifier
Deliverable: CDC-ready package · Issued to certifier
§ 02.4

Construction certificates

Post-DA documentation to obtain a Construction Certificate. The drawings step up in detail — every dimension, every finish, every detail the builder needs and the certifier checks — while honouring the conditions of consent.

If the DA was lodged by another party, we can pick up the project at CC stage and produce the documentation set from the approved drawings forward.

  • Conditions of consent review and resolution
  • Issue-for-construction drawing set
  • Detailed dimensions, finishes, and joinery
  • BCA compliance statements
  • Coordination with structural, hydraulic, and electrical consultants
Deliverable: CC documentation set · Issued to certifier
§ 02.5

NSW Planning Portal lodgement

The portal is its own quiet bottleneck. We manage the full lodgement workflow — case creation, document upload, fee schedule, RFI response — so a documentation package doesn't sit in draft for a fortnight waiting on form fields.

Engaged on its own for clients who have their own drawings but want lodgement handled cleanly, or rolled into any of the documentation services above.

  • NSW Planning Portal case creation and management
  • Document preparation to portal naming conventions
  • Owner's consent, cost estimate, and statutory declarations
  • Council and certifier liaison
  • RFI response and re-submission
Deliverable: Lodged application · Portal case reference
§ 02.6

BCA / NCC compliance

Standalone NCC 2022 reports — class assessment, fire and egress, accessibility under AS 1428.1, sanitary provisions, food premises against AS 4674. Issued before lodgement to flag the things that hold up a CC.

Useful where a builder or designer has a set of drawings and needs an independent compliance read before committing to the approval pathway.

  • Building classification and characteristics
  • Fire safety, egress, and travel distance assessment
  • Accessibility under AS 1428.1
  • Food premises against AS 4674 — 2004
  • Performance Solutions where Deemed-to-Satisfy is not feasible
Deliverable: NCC compliance report · Issued as PDF
